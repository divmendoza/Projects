Liam Payne, born on August 29, 1993, in Wolverhampton, England,
is a singer and songwriter who gained fame as a member of
One Direction. Known for his deep voice, he contributed to the band's
global success with five albums and numerous hits. After the
band's hiatus in 2016, Liam pursued a solo career, releasing popular
singles like "Strip That Down."

Harry Styles, born on February 1, 1994, in Redditch, England, is a singer,
songwriter, and actor who rose to fame as a member of One Direction.
With his distinctive voice and charismatic stage presence, he helped
drive the band's success with five albums and numerous hits. After the
band's hiatus in 2016, Harry launched a successful solo career, releasing
acclaimed albums and singles like "Sign of the Times."

Zayn Malik, born on January 12, 1993, is a British singer and songwriter
who rose to fame with the boy band One Direction. Known for his
soulful voice, Zayn left the band in 2015 to pursue a successful solo
career. His debut album, "Mind of Mine," featuring the hit single
"Pillowtalk," topped charts worldwide. With his striking looks, unique
fashion sense, and impressive vocal range, Zayn continues to captivate audiences in the music industry.
