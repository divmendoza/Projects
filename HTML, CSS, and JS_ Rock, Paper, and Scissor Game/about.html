Welcome to Clash of Hands! The
timeless game of chance and strategy! The rules

scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock.
Whether you're looking to kill a few minutes
or challenge the computer, our version of this
classic game brings the fun to your screen
with a smooth and engaging experience.
Test your reflexes, hone your decision-making
skills, and see if you can outsmart your opponent
in this quick and exciting game!
